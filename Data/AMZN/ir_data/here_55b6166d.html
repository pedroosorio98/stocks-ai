ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The AWS, CrowdStrike and Nvidia Cybersecurity Accelerator is a 8-week equity-free program for seed-stage startups specifically designed to support companies developing cybersecurity technologies in the following categories:

Cloud Security

Application Security

Identity

Agentic Security

Data Security

Other

Participation Benefits

Showcase at AWS re-Inforce 2026

Winner of the demo day at RSA will have the chance to showcase at CrowdStrike's booth at AWS re-inforce 26

GTM Support

Co-sell workshops, 1:1 mentoring with experts, Marketplace and APN support to accelerate growth

Cloud Support

1:1 mentoring sessions with AWS Solution Architects and access to specialized resources and industry specialists

CISO Mentors

Access to CISO mentors providing strategic, experience-driven guidance and support

Marketing & PR

Co-marketing resources and media coverage for selected startups

Pitching Session

Pitching opportunity to investors and ecosystem players through Demo Day

Ecosystem Engagement

Access to wide network of community, VC funds, compliance, technology & innovation experts and more!

Fundraising Support

Become funding-ready with hands-on workshops. Validate your strategy, pitch deck and storytelling and get access to leading VCs

Alumni Community

The community brings together all program members, including alumni founders for both CEOs and CTOs

Up to 70% Off

Participants and applicants are eligible to receive up to 70% off on Falcon Go and Pro
