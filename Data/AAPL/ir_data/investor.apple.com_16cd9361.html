2026 Annual Meeting of Shareholders

Apple will host the 2026 Annual Meeting of Shareholders on February 24, 2026 at 8:00 am P.T. in a virtual format. You can access the meeting by visiting www.virtualshareholdermeeting.com/AAPL2026 on the day of the meeting. The bank, broker, or other organization that holds your Apple shares will be issuing proxy materials to you that will include aunique control number. You’ll need that unique control number to access the meeting, and vote during the meeting. The record date for the meeting is January 2, 2026. Additional details about the meeting and the matters to be voted on are available in our proxy statement.
