Complaints

To report financial transactions or external suspicious of scam/fraud (involving PIX transactions, wire transfers, payment slips, or cards) or be served at a branch, access the Fale Conosco web page or contact SAC Alô Bradesco, available 24/7, by calling 800 704 8383. Whistleblowing

The Corporate Whistleblowing Channel was designed to receive reports of violations of the Organization’s Code of Ethical Conduct, Policies and Standards, and other unlawful acts like internal fraud, money laundering, corruption, conflicts of interest, and moral or sexual harassment, among other misconduct committed by Bradesco employees or linked companies.

 

How to make a report?

You have two options to make a report: through our online form and by calling 800 776 4820, from Monday through Friday, from 8:00 am to 6:00 pm, except for weekends and Brazilian holidays.

 

Important details

Reports can be made anonymously and will always be handled with confidentiality. When making a report, it is fundamentally important to provide detailed information and, if possible, submit evidence like documents or photos. After making a report, you can keep track of it using the generated case number.

Bradesco e a Conformidade com Leis de Proteção de Dados

O Bradesco declara que cumpre toda a legislação aplicável sobre segurança da informação, privacidade e proteção de dados, inclusive a Constituição Federal, o Código de Defesa do Consumidor, o Código Civil, o Marco Civil da Internet (Lei Federal nº 12.965/2014), seu decreto regulamentador (Decreto 8.771/2016), a Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (Lei Federal nº 13.709/2018), e demais normas setoriais ou gerais sobre o tema. Nos comprometemos a tratar os dados pessoais coletados neste formulário somente nos estritos limites e finalidades aqui previstos. Caso tenha dúvidas sobre a forma do tratamento dos seus dados pessoais, consulte as informações disponíveis no endereço eletrônico LGPD | Bradesco Segurança (banco.bradesco).
