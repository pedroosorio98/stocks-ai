    Sustainability Report 2023

    Mining for Tomorrow: Sustaining Operations with Responsibility

    About the Theme

    In supporting sustainability amidst low-carbon economic needs, PT Vale is committed to conducting long-term operational activities by implementing sustainable practices. We continue to innovate and invest in environmentally friendly technologies to minimize the negative impacts of mining activities.

    More about our Purpose
    click the + button to expand and learn more about our report

    Dear respected stakeholders,

    PT Vale recognizes the urgency of implementing Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles for the future of the domestic industry. Every decision we make is carefully considered for its impact on ESG, as we believe that sustainable mining can significantly contribute to the well-being of the earth and society.

    We appreciate the support from all stakeholders in our efforts to implement sustainable practices. Together, we are committed to building a greener and more sustainable future for generations to come.
     

    Febriany Eddy

    CEO & President Director PT Vale Indonesia Tbk

    PT Vale's Sustainability Report is issued based on and in reference to Law No. 40 of 2007 and Financial Services Authority (OJK) Regulation No. 51/POJK.03/2007.

    The preparation of the report adheres to the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) 2021 Standards, Sustainability Reporting Guidelines & Mining and Metals Sector Supplement (MMSS) GRI-4, and Sustainability Accounting Standards Board (SASB) Metals and Mining 2021

    Determination of Material Topics


    We have prepared the Sustainability Report in compliance with existing laws and regulations, namely Law No. 40 of 20There have been no changes in the material topics from the previous reporting period. Material topics are determined based on surveys and discussions involving internal and external stakeholders. Discussions are facilitated by an independent party as the Expert Team.

    The discussion results in 10 material topics relevant to the sustainability context and aligned with support for TPB achievement.

    Low Carbon Agenda

    To reduce carbon emissions, PT Vale is focusing on implementing renewable energy (EBT) with a target of reducing carbon emissions by up to 33% by 2030. This development project has undergone comprehensive risk management processes, including social and environmental aspects.

    In 2023, the Company initiated the construction of a Nursery, or breeding center, for the Indonesia Growth Project (IGP) in Pomalaa, Kolaka Regency, Southeast Sulawesi. This Nursery facility is prepared for post-mining reclamation purposes in the Pomalaa Block and other areas in Kolaka.

    Reducing Landfill Loads Together


    We are committed to comprehensive and sustainable environmental management. PT Vale is committed to achieving zero waste to landfill by 2025, five years ahead of the government’s target. 

    To achieve the zero waste to landfill target, We implemented several supporting activities during 2023, include but not limited to utilized organic waste as feed for maggots and organic compost and community guidance on waste bank management.

    Reducing Water Consumption Intensity

    The commitment to reduce water consumption intensity is realized through the implementation of the Water Efficiency and the Reduction of Wastewater Load Policies. Water usage efficiency is carried out in factory areas, mining sites, offices, and other supporting facilities by 1% per year.

    The amount of water consumed is equal to the water withdrawn from the lake, and no water is taken from water-stressed areas.

    We aim to continue our presence and contribute positively to the community, including creating more job opportunities. We are committed to maximizing  local employment and enhancing  competencies through workforce training.
     
    We also provides extensive opportunities for women to take on roles and maximize their capabilities within the operational areas. At PT Vale, we are committed to providing equal opportunities for all individuals to work and advance their careers.

    Together Empowering the Communities

    PT Vale has conducted a social impact evaluation of the activities carried out by the Company in the Sorowako, Bahodopi, and Pomalaa areas. PT Vale has also conducted a Social Return on Investment (SROI) analysis in Sorowako. In 2023, PT Vale's social programs contributed to achieving the Green PROPER rating, especially in community empowerment, and have been recognized by various relevant institutions.

    PT Vale's mining activities are carried out in an integrated manner, encompassing not only extraction but also processing.

    The extension of the company's contract into a Special Mining Business License (IUPK) was conducted through a Divestment Commitment between PT Vale and PT Mineral Industri Indonesia (Persero), or MIND ID.
