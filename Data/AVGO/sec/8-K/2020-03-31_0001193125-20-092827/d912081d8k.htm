Item 5.07. Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.

On March 30, 2020, Broadcom Inc. (the “Company”) held its 2020 Annual Meeting of Stockholders. Holders of the Company’s shares of common stock voted on the following matters:

(1) To elect 9 members to the Company’s board of directors;

(2) To ratify the appointment of PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP as the Company’s independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending November 1, 2020;

(3) Non-binding, advisory vote to approve the compensation of the Company’s named executive officers.
