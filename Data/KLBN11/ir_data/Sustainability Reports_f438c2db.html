Sustainability Report

Highlights of the Company's performance and challennges in 2024 towards KSDGs.

Environmental 

Risk management and impact mitigation related to climate change, as well as biodiversity care, and the responsible use of natural resources are priorities in Klabin’s environmental management. The Company advances in these areas by adopting internationally recognized practices, seeking new solutions, and engaging its employees in sustainability goals. 

link

Slider1 US - 2024

Scope 3 emissions reduction
Between 2022 and 2024, Klabin reduced its scope 3 greenhouse gas emissions by 17.4%, supported by the Value Chain Engagement Program. The Company encourages its partners to assess their own impacts and pursue process decarbonization.

LEARN MORE
Innovation in products
The PineFluff® and PineFluff eXcel® solutions have a carbon footprint up to 64% lower than other types of fluff pulp, expanding the portfolio of products with a positive impact.

LEARN MORE
Biodiversity
Klabin published its Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services Conservation Plan, aiming to achieve a net biodiversity gain by 2050. The Company’s actions include monitoring fauna and flora, rewilding initiatives, and controlling invasive exotic species in conservation areas.

LEARN MORE
Animal Welfare Certification
Parque Ecológico Klabin (Klabin Ecological Park) received international certification for its care of wildlife species. With this achievement, Klabin became the only pulp and paper company to receive this award.

LEARN MORE

Social

Klabin’s social initiatives encompass education, culture and citizenship, development, and environment areas. With a focus on local development, the Company maintains structured programs in communities near its operations. 

link

Slider2 - US 2024

Território Circular Program
Supported cooperatives experienced an average income increase of 86%, strengthening the circular economy and productive inclusion in the regions where Klabin operates.

LEARN MORE
Semeando Educação Program
Operating in four states, the program benefited more than 28,000 students by promoting improvements in school management and learning.

LEARN MORE
Public management and infrastructure
In 2024, 83% of the actions planned by the municipalities participating in the Klabin Transforma – Apoio à Gestão Pública Program were aligned with the Social Progress Index. Over 1,000 actions were planned and monitored across 14 municipalities.

LEARN MORE
Centro de Qualificação Profissional Klabin
Klabin Center for Professional Development, opened in 2024 in the municipality of Telêmaco Borba, offers ongoing training for employees and opportunities for the entire community, strengthening the commitment to developing the local workforce.

LEARN MORE

Governance 

Klabin maintains governance practices based on integrity, transparency, and risk management. Technical committees, updated policies, and indicator monitoring strengthen corporate governance and the ethical conduct of business. 

link

Slider3 - US 2024

Dow Jones highlight
Klabin achieved its highest historical score on the Dow Jones Best-in-Class Indices (DJSI), with 88 points, solidifying its position among the global leaders in corporate sustainability.

LEARN MORE
Leadership recognition
Klabin’s CEO was named Executive of the Year in Latin America, demonstrating the Company’s leading role in the ESG agenda.

LEARN MORE
Transparency
For the first time, Klabin disclosed cash cost and capex projections, reinforcing the predictability of the Company’s business model.

LEARN MORE
Risk control
Completion of the SoftExpert system implementation drives the standardization and revision of documents and policies.

Klabin 125 years 

In 2024, Klabin celebrates 125 years of a history of leadership in the paper and cellulose sector. From forests to paper packaging, the Company has reinvented itself and stood out for the excellence of its processes, product quality, and diversified portfolio.
